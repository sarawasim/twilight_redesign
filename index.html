COVID-19 Update - December 8, 2020

Each showing is limited to 50 vehicles.
Single feature admissions only.
Gates open 20 minutes prior to the start of showing.
Please stay inside your vehicles and not congregate together.
If you feel ill or were outiside of Canada within the last 14 days, please stay home.

Please note: The concession is temporarily closed.

Washroom Rules

  • Washrooms are limited to 6 people at a time
  • Please wait outside 2m apart
  • Face masks are required in the washrooms

The washrooms are cleaned and sanitized regularly throughout the evening. Please consider using the washrooms while the show is on to reduce crowds.
